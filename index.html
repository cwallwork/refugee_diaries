  • Why I'm going

    I love what I do. Every day I bring stories to life and give voice to great characters. I play Bonnie Bennett, in the series, “The Vampire Diaries.” It’s a dream job.

    Of course, the roles that I play aren’t real – they are the product of great writing and imagination. But there are real stories in the world that I know are important, and that I want to help tell. Stories that nobody is talking about. Stories that need to be told.

    No story is more important to me than the story of refugees.

The Issue

88% of families detained in the U.S. after fleeing Central America pass the "credible fear" test.

in 2014, 81% of children fleeing with their families were 12 years or younger.

3/4 children arriving at the U.S. border come from Guatemala, El Salvador and Honduras, known as the 'Northern Triangle'.

The Northern Triangle has the highest homicide rate in the world.

$$$$

Over 50% of the population in the Northern Triangle live on less than $4 a day.

100000

In 2014, upwards of 140,000 women and children fled their homes for safety, protection and basic human rights.
